VARANASI one of India's oldest and holiest cities, is a vibrant tapestry of spirituality, history, and culture. Revered as a major pilgrimage site, it is renowned for its ghats along the Ganges River, where ancient rituals and lively ceremonies take place. The city's narrow alleys are lined with temples, bustling markets, and traditional eateries, offering a sensory feast of sights, sounds, and flavors.
