LUCKNOW the capital city of Uttar Pradesh, is a vibrant blend of historical grandeur and modernity. Known as the "City of Nawabs," it boasts an array of architectural marvels such as the Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, and the Rumi Darwaza. The city is also renowned for its delectable Awadhi cuisine, offering delights like kebabs, biryanis, and rich desserts.
