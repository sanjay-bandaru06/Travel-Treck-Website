MAHARASHTRA
Maharashtra, located in the western region of India, is the country's second-most populous state and third-largest by area. It is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vibrant cities. The capital city, Mumbai, is the financial hub of India and home to Bollywood, the largest film industry in the world. Maharashtra boasts numerous UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Ajanta and Ellora Caves, which are renowned for their ancient rock-cut architecture and intricate sculptures.
