ANDHRA PRADESH
Andhra Pradesh is located in the southeastern part of India, bordered by Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Odisha, and the Bay of Bengal. The state's capital is Amaravati, and its largest city is Visakhapatnam.
TAMIL NADU
Tamil Nadu is located in the southern part of India, bordered by Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and the Bay of Bengal. Its capital city is Chennai, known for its cultural heritage and bustling economy.
UTTAR PRADESH
Uttar Pradesh is situated in the northern part of India, bordered by Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Delhi, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, and Nepal. Its capital is Lucknow, and it is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance.
MAHARASHTRA
Maharashtra is located in the western part of India, bordered by Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana, Karnataka, Goa, and the Arabian Sea. Its capital city is Mumbai, which is also the financial and entertainment hub of India.
JAMMU & KASHMIR
Jammu and Kashmir is located in the northernmost part of India, bordered by Himachal Pradesh and Punjab to the south and shares international borders with Pakistan and China. The region is known for its stunning landscapes, including the Kashmir Valley
DELHI
Delhi, the capital territory of India, is located in the northern part of the country. It is bordered by Haryana on three sides and Uttar Pradesh to the east, encompassing the bustling city of New Delhi, which serves as the nation's capital and the seat of all three branches of the Indian government.
