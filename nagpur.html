NAGPUR Nagpur, often referred to as the "Orange City" for its extensive orange orchards, is a major city in Maharashtra, India. It serves as a prominent commercial and political center, with its strategic location in the heart of India making it a key transportation hub. Nagpur is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and landmarks such as the Deekshabhoomi and the Sitabuldi Fort
