REDFORT Lal Qila, in Delhi, India, was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan between 1638 and 1648. This iconic fort served as the Mughal emperors' main residence for nearly 200 years. Renowned for its red sandstone walls and exquisite Mughal architecture, it houses key structures like the Lahori Gate, Diwan-i-Aam, and Diwan-i-Khas.
