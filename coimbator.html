COIMBATORoften referred to as the "Manchester of South India," is a vibrant city located in the state of Tamil Nadu. Known for its thriving textile industry, the city has a rich industrial heritage and is a major hub for manufacturing and engineering. Coimbatore is also renowned for its pleasant climate, thanks to its location near the Western Ghats. The city boasts a blend of traditional and modern elements, with ancient temples such as Marudamalai Temple and contemporary attractions like Codissia Trade Fair Complex.
