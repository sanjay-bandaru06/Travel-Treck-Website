JAMMU & KASHMIR
Jammu & Kashmir is a newly created Union Territory in India consisting of two divisions: Jammu Division & Kashmir Division, both of which are administered by the Central Government of India. It is located to the north of Himachal Pradesh & Punjab and to the west of Ladakh. Jammu is known as the City of Temples & offers plentiful sightseeing opportunities with its gardens, palaces, forts & religious attractions, the most famous of which is Mata Vaishno Devi in Katra.
ROGAN JOSH
Rogan josh is a fragrant lamb curry from Kashmir, India. Tender pieces of lamb are simmered in a flavorful sauce made with yogurt, aromatic spices, and Kashmiri chilies, giving it a vibrant red color.

MODUR PULAV
Modur Pulao is a sweet rice dish originating from Kashmir, India. It's a fragrant and flavorful rice dish loaded with nuts, dried fruits, and spices. The word "Modur" translates to "sweet" in Kashmiri, perfectly reflecting the taste profile of this celebratory dish.

YAKHNI
Yakhni is a slow-cooked stew or curry from Kashmir, India. Traditionally made with lamb, it features tender meat simmered in a yogurt-based broth flavored with aromatic spices like cardamom and fennel seeds.

MATSCHGAND
Matschgand is a Kashmiri delight for meat lovers. These are savory minced meatballs, formed into cylinders and cooked in a rich, flavorful red gravy. Packed with warming spices, the dish is a staple in Kashmir's cuisine.

AMARNATH
Amarnath is a sacred Hindu cave shrine located high in the Himalayas of Jammu and Kashmir, India. Enshrined within the glacial cave is a naturally formed ice lingam, a symbolic representation of Lord Shiva.

RAGHUNATH
The Raghunath Temple is a Hindu temple complex in Jammu, India, dedicated to Lord Rama, also known as Raghunath. It's one of the largest temple complexes in North India and a prominent landmark in Jammu.

AVANTIPUR
Avantipur is a historical city in ruins, located in Kashmir, India. Once a thriving capital city during the 6th-8th centuries AD, it's known for its ancient temples dedicated to Shiva and Vishnu.

VAISHNO DEVI
Vaishno Devi, also referred to as Mata Rani, Trikuta, Ambe, and Vaishnavi, is a revered Hindu manifestation of the Mother Goddess. Devotees believe her to be a combined avatar of the goddesses Mahakali, Mahalakshmi, and Mahasarasvati.

GULMARG
Gulmarg, a picturesque town in Jammu and Kashmir, India, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and as a premier destination for skiing and winter sports. Nestled in the Pir Panjal range, it features lush green meadows in summer and snow-covered slopes in winter.

LADAKH
Ladakh, located in the northernmost region of India, is renowned for its stunning landscapes, including arid mountains, clear blue lakes, and pristine valleys. It is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts, offering activities like trekking, river rafting, and biking.
