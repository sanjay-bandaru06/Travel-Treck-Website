DELHI
Delhi, India's vibrant capital, blends ancient history with modernity. Renowned for its historical landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate, the city showcases a rich cultural heritage. Its bustling markets, such as Chandni Chowk, offer a variety of traditional delicacies and shopping experiences. Modern attractions include the Lotus Temple and the Akshardham Temple, reflecting Delhi's architectural diversity. The city is a political hub, housing the Parliament and government offices. Delhi's extensive metro system connects its various neighborhoods, enhancing urban mobility.
